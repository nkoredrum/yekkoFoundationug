Our Healthcare Centers

Providing quality healthcare services to communities in Masaka and surrounding districts

Yekko Medical Centre

Our main medical facility providing comprehensive healthcare services including general medicine, pediatrics, and specialized care. Located in Masaka City, we serve patients from all seven districts.

  • General Medicine
  • Pediatrics
  • Specialized Care
  • Emergency Services

Yekko Dental Clinic

State-of-the-art dental facility offering comprehensive dental care services. Our experienced dental team provides both preventive and curative dental treatments.

  • Preventive Dentistry
  • Restorative Care
  • Orthodontics
  • Emergency Dental Care

Yekko Eye Care Centre

Specialized eye care facility providing comprehensive vision care services. We offer diagnosis, treatment, and management of various eye conditions.

  • Vision Testing
  • Eye Disease Treatment
  • Surgical Procedures
  • Vision Rehabilitation
