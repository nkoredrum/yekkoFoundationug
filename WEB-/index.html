About Us

Our Mission

The mission of Yekko Foundation Uganda is to improve the health, well-being, and quality of life of vulnerable communities in Uganda through sustainable healthcare initiatives, education, and community empowerment.

Our Vision

The vision of Yekko Foundation Uganda is to create a society where every individual has access to quality healthcare, education, and economic opportunities, enabling them to live a dignified and fulfilling life.

Our Projects

Healthcare

Providing access to quality medical and health services, promoting health awareness to reduce the risk of diseases and to address disease burden in rural areas of Uganda

Yekko Medical Centre

Our main medical facility providing comprehensive healthcare services including general medicine, pediatrics, and specialized care. Located in Masaka City, we serve patients from all seven districts.

  • General Medicine
  • Pediatrics
  • Specialized Care
  • Emergency Services

Yekko Dental Clinic

State-of-the-art dental facility offering comprehensive dental care services. Our experienced dental team provides both preventive and curative dental treatments.

  • Preventive Dentistry
  • Restorative Care
  • Orthodontics
  • Emergency Dental Care

Yekko Eye Care Centre

Specialized eye care facility providing comprehensive vision care services. We offer diagnosis, treatment, and management of various eye conditions.

  • Vision Testing
  • Eye Disease Treatment
  • Surgical Procedures
  • Vision Rehabilitation
